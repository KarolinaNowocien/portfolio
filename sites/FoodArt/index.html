About Food

"The preparation of good food is merely another expression of art, one of the joys of civilized living"

Hello welcome to our blog, we are food photographers based in Wroclaw, Poland. Our passion is food and photography. Our day to day passion involves working with food and photography, in-between shoots we create recipes and look for inspiration from chefs, photographers, family an friends. We even leave the studio to go on the odd adventure from time to time. We have a passion for food and photography, we hope you can find some inspiration here through our photographic processes the recipes we recreate and develop and the everyday things that drives our vision.Food-project started in January 2016 and is growing every day! Creating beauty and discovering tastes is our top priority! Let us know if you like it, suggest us or just say hello!
